**Men's Companion 0.2.0** — first public release.

A faster, app-native front-end for the **a.intimstory.ltd** directory, with a photo-dedup intelligence layer that tells you which different cards are actually the same person. Listings and profiles are fetched live from the site and parsed on your device using your own account; a backend adds only the match intelligence (it never serves card content).

### Highlights
- **Find same / Related** — other cards sharing the *same photos* (with how many photos match and how close), and cards sharing the *same phone* across the whole site.
- **Card-density heatmap** of the city — tap a cell to list the cards there.
- **Search** by phone / ID, by parameters (age, bust, height, weight, price), and by **metro / area** (with optional neighbouring stations).
- **Client-side filters** — status, price, age, bust, height, weight, location.
- **Per-card map** showing the distance from you.
- **Saved list, reviews & scoring** — tied to your site account.
- **Full-screen photo viewer** with zoom, plus extra-payment service annotations and price details on each card.

### Install
1. Download `mens-companion-0.2.0.apk` below.
2. Open it on your phone and allow installing from your browser / file manager.
3. Requires **Android 8.0+** (API 26).
4. Sign in with your **a.intimstory.ltd** account to use the saved list and reviews.

### Notes
- Your credentials go **directly to the site**, not to any third-party server.
- The directory's content always comes live from the site — the app does not host or copy it.

*Source code is not published; this repository hosts the release only.*
